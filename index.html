Crystals Collector

Instructions

You are given a target number at the start of the game. Each crystal has a randomly assigned value that is hidden from you until you click on it. Clicking on a crystal adds the crystals value to your total score. You win the game if your total score matches the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. Good luck!
